We are the consulting company for entreprenuerships and SMEs.

Who we are

Unique Consulting provides legal and business consulting services to entrepreneurs and small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Colombia.

The Team

What we do

Our goal is to nourish and boost the growth of entrepreneurship projects and SMEs. Through our “UNIQUE” model, we are able to centralize legal and business consulting services for our clients.

Legal Consulting

Corporate

We advise our clients on the incorporation of companies, corporate structuring, corporate governance and drafting, and reviewing of commercial and civil contracts.

Transactional Matters

We advise our clients on due diligence processes, and mergers and acquisitions (M&A).

Competition and Antitrust

We advise our clients on competition matters, including business integrations and anticompetitive and unfair trade practices

Asesoría Empresarial

Business Plan Development

We help our clients to develop and structure their business plan in a personalized way, in order to maximize their opportunities. Additionally, we have a wide network of expert allies in different business matters that allows us to adjust our services to their needs.

Financial Advising

We analyze the needs of our clients and their capital structure in order to find the best fit for their financing requirements, either through financial institutions, family and friends, or investor angels.

Brand Conception & Corporate Identity

We advise our clients in the creation of their brand and their corporate identity.

Experience

Our team has vast experience in mergers, aquisitions, financing, capital markets, trading, and risk management in Latin America.

Our partners are involved in the design and implementation of your business needs every step of the way.
